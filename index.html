About Us

Transforming Agriculture, Empowering Communities

At HAGS, we believe in the power of agricultural diversification to transform rural economies. Our mission is to provide high-quality Hass avocado seedlings and expert agricultural knowledge to help communities thrive.

We work primarily with rural community groups, offering not just seedlings but comprehensive support including planting techniques, care guidelines, and market connections. Our holistic approach ensures that farmers can maximize their yields and profits.

Joseph Kungu Ndungu

Founder & Agricultural Expert
