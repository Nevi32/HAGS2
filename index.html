Our Story

Growing Together

Founded by agricultural expert Joseph Kungu Ndungu, Hass Avocado Growers (HAGS) was born from a vision to transform rural communities through sustainable agricultural practices.

We specialize in cultivating premium Hass avocado seedlings, working directly with community groups to diversify their agricultural output and create sustainable income streams.

Our approach is not just about selling seedlings; it's about building partnerships, providing knowledge, and supporting the entire growth journey from planting to harvest.

Meet Our Founder

Joseph Kungu Ndungu

With over 15 years of experience in agricultural science and community development, Joseph founded HAGS with a vision to transform rural agriculture through sustainable practices and high-value crops.

A certified agricultural expert specializing in fruit tree cultivation, Joseph has worked with numerous community groups across the country, sharing his knowledge and passion for sustainable farming.

His approach combines traditional wisdom with modern agricultural techniques, ensuring that HAGS seedlings are not only of the highest quality but also suited to local growing conditions.
